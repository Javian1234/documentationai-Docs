---
title: Can't Submit a Report
description: Fix issues preventing report submissions.
---

## Symptoms

- "Submit" button is disabled
- No confirmation after submitting an issue

## Common Causes

- Missing required fields (photo/location/description)
- Internet connectivity problems

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check required fields">
    Ensure all form fields are filled, especially location and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh and retry">
    Reload the page and resubmit your report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test your connection">
    Make sure your device is connected to the internet.
  </Step>
</Steps>
