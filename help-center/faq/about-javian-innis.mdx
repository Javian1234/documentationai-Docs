## About Javian Innis

Javian Innis is the founder of Fix-it-Jamaica.

His mission is to empower Jamaicans to improve local infrastructure by making it easy to report and track issues in their communities.

## Vision

Javian champions a collaborative approach to community improvement, helping citizens connect with local authorities and each other.

## Role

He leads the team and oversees platform development, ensuring Fix-it-Jamaica remains simple and effective for everyone.
