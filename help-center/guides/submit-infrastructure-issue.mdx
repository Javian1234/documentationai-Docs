## Report a Problem

Use Fix-it-Jamaica to help improve your community.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your account">
    Visit the Fix-it-Jamaica website and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Report Issue'">
    Start a new submission from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the form">
    Add a description, select the location, and upload a photo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and track">
    Submit your report. You can monitor updates from your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
